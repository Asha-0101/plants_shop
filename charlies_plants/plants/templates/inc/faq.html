Proper plant care depends on the type of plant you have. Here are some general tips:
  • Watering: Most plants prefer consistent watering. Overwatering can lead to root rot, while underwatering can cause wilting. Check the soil moisture before watering.
  • Light: Ensure your plant gets the right amount of light. Some plants thrive in bright, indirect light, while others prefer low-light conditions.
  • Humidity: Tropical plants often require higher humidity. You can mist them regularly or use a humidifier.
  • Fertilizing: Use a balanced fertilizer during the growing season to promote healthy growth.
  • Pruning: Regularly trim dead or yellowing leaves to encourage new growth.
For specific care instructions, refer to the care guide provided with your plant or contact our support team.
